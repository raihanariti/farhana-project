Hello!
I'm FARHANA RAHMAN.

I used to be a chemist, and then a teacher, and now I make my living as a freelancer. If you want to know a lot more about me and how I got here, you can read about my journey by clicking here. Feel free to write me at somethingaboutmaps@gmail.com to ask me about my favorite typefaces, why I dislike the Van der Grinten projection, or to hire me to help you tell your spatial stories.

and I make maps.

It seems to me that maps are as much about art as data, and creating connections between people and stories that happen to have a geography. I worry that the speed and ease of the computer has made it too easy to leave the humanity out of maps — that creative spark that people bring to their unfeeling tools.
